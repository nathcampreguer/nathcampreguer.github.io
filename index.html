About

Hi! I am a researcher interested in understanding how people appropriate tools, materials and techniques in making - and in counterpart, how designs can create space for appropriation through interaction design (IxD). In my work, I am committed to implementing methodologies and methods that foster diversity.

I am currently a PhD student at the University of Salzburg, Austria.
