About

Hi! I am a researcher interested in understanding how people appropriate tools, materials and techniques in making - and in counterpart, how designs can create space for appropriation through interaction design (IxD). I am committed to implementing methodologies and methods that foster diversity. In my PhD, I look into feminist theory and how it can contribute to the discourse around intersectionality in IxD.

I am currently a research fellow and PhD student at the University of Salzburg, Austria.
